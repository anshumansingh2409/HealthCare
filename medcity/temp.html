About Us

We are a tech-for-good startup bringing life-saving healthcare to rural India through AI, blockchain, and compassion. Our offline-ready app enables locals to self-diagnose, consult doctors virtually, and access reliable medicines.
Our work began in a village with no doctor. Today, we serve 20+ remote communities with the help of local health workers and smart AI.

Our Mission

  • Make healthcare accessible to every Indian citizen.
  • Use AI to bridge the doctor-patient gap in remote areas.
  • Educate people on health and hygiene through smart tools.
  • Ensure privacy, affordability, and real-time care.
